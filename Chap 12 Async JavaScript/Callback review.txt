Async != Sync

Sync => We may execute only one instruction(a line of code) at a time

  ex: console.log("HI");

Async => We may execute 2 or more instruction at a time


  EX: Displaying the time at the TypingUP

      setInterval(()=>{
        console.log(time);
      }, 1000);
